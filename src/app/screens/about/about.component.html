More on Us

Big Data Big Impact (BDBI) at Georgia Tech's College of Computing facilitates diverse, full-stack, data- intensive projects students work together on over a year. BDBI aligns each project with social good themes based on U.N. sustainability goals.

BDBI's projects focus on analysis and machine learning, platform and data infrastructure, and finally, data visualization. Some of BDBI's notable projects include Wildfire Risk Detection, Fake News Detection, Solar Forecasting, and MARTA route analysis.
